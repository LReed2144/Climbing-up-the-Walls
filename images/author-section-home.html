Inside the Book

About Camille Pandian Milner

Camille Pandian Milner draws on her years steeped in London’s 2000s music scene to bring the novel’s world to life — blending the city’s eclectic sounds, late-night energy, and unforgettable characters into a story that feels authentically lived-in.
